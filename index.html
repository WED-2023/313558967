About Me

Hello, I'm Maria Simkin

Born in the 1930s in the Soviet Union, my life has been a journey of resilience and love. Despite facing antisemitism in my childhood, I pursued education fervently and became a distinguished dentist after graduating from the top medical school in the Soviet Union.

I've cherished family deeply, raising four children with my late husband and embracing a blended family later in life. In 1990, we moved to Israel, settling in Eilat where we became the first Russians to call it home. Our pioneering spirit infused the community with vibrancy and diversity.

Today, Eilat is not just a city; it's our cherished home. Join me as I reflect on a life filled with love, laughter, and adventure. This website is a tribute to the journey that shaped me, a testament to the resilience and courage that define my story.

Feel free to reach out to me via email below. I invite you to share your questions, stories, and thoughts.

With warm regards,
Maria Simkin

1. Key Differences between Web1, Web2, and Web3

Web1: Early internet era with static web pages and limited user interaction.

Web2: Interactive web with dynamic content and user-generated contributions.

Web3: Decentralized web focused on blockchain technology and user empowerment.

Key Differences between Web1, Web2, and Web3 for car purchase

Web1: Users visit static car dealership websites to browse models, specs, and prices. They then visit the dealership in person to finalize the purchase.

Web2: Users engage with interactive dealership websites, read reviews, and compare prices online before visiting a dealership for the purchase.

Web3: Users utilize blockchain-powered platforms for transparent peer-to-peer car trading, accessing ownership history and maintenance records securely online.
